Kuangchen Lu

I am a undergraduate student major in bioinformatics. Although my works are mostly about bioinformatics, I love computer science even more. I am interested in program transformation, human-computer interaction, typesetting and  programming education.

Projects

WebPhyloTree

Homepage: http://kelvinlu1024.github.io/WebPhyloTree

This is a JavaScript library that helps visualizing interactive phylogenetic trees in HTML and SVG. It can display trees in all three popular layouts (rectangular, circular and unrooted).

Scheme Interpreter in R5RS Scheme

Homepage: https://github.com/KelvinLu1024/Simple-Scheme-Interpreter

This is my first experiment on implementing call-with-current-continuation (or call/cc). This program utilizes the concept of continuation-passing style.

A Brief Programming Tutorial (in Chinese)

Read here: https://github.com/KelvinLu1024/Programming-Tutorial-Chinese/blob/master/tutorial.ipynb

I wrote this tutorial for my girlfriend to help her understand the basic concepts in computer programming. I hope this material could also help others.
